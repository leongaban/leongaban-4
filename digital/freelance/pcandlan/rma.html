RMA POLICY

1. No return or exchange after 15 days from date of shipment for non-defective items. Returns of non-defective items or due to customer's error are subject to up to 20% re-stocking fee.

2. Misships must be claimed within 10 days from date of shipment.

3. Defective items may be returned for repair or replacement within 15 days from shipment. After 15 days, the defective items can be repaired or replaced by the manufacturers of the item within the warranty period of the product. All items sold are new unless specifically noted. Most items have at least a one-year warranty through the manufacturer. In some cases, we will repair or replace items that are dead/defective on arrival (DOA). We will help you contact the manufacturer for warranty repair or replacement or you can contact them directly.

4. No return on software, printhead, or media (papers, labels, ribbons, etc.). No return or exchange for all international orders. All sales are final once orders are shipped out. Defective software (broken cd) can only be exchanged within policy period.

5. No return on discontinued items or selective products per manufacturer policy.

6. Customer is responsible for the freight for return or exchange.

7. All return or exchange without a valid RMA# is not accepted. RMA # is valid for 15 days from date of issuance. The RMA number must be visible on all returns.

8. All return or exchange items must be in brand new, resalable condition with all original packaging and accessories for credit to be issued. Do not write on the original product box. Double box is recommended. Otherwise, additional charge may apply or result in refusal.
