Description: WRNO Worldwide, a division of Good News World Outreach, a non-profit organization based in the Dallas/Fort Worth Metroplex.
   
Mission
Statement:		 WRNO Worldwide short wave radio station is dedicated to providing news, advertising, and educational content programming internationally to enhance the whole person.
   

Service
/
Product:

 WRNO Worldwide has an international short wave commercial license to broadcast globally. Currently, there are 27 short wave licenses issued by the FCC. Of these, WRNO Worldwide is the only station that is focusing on a total spectrum of “education” instead of being designated as primarily “religious or commercial.” The call letters WRNO and its three frequencies have been in use since 1983 and are established as prime providers of broadcasting services worldwide. 
   
Market Analysis: WRNO Worldwide has a listener’s footprint of ¾ of the globe (Review Appendix A – Listeners Map at Full Power).

Potentially, billions of people can be reached daily, as short wave radio broadcasting has global access, ease of use, and requires only a short wave radio or receiver. Richard Schneider, co-founder of Radio for Peace International states …"Short wave will be good for another 20 or 30 years all over the world. There are a lot of other ways to get news, but not a lot of ways to disseminate news in rural India.” With one short wave broadcast millions of people can be reached with the message on a daily basis.
