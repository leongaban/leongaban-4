  Good News World Outreach was founded by Robert and Janet Mawire. Pastor Robert has 25 years in global ministry. His work has been featured on the 700 Club, NBC, 20/20, and the American Journal, as well as numerous newspapers and magazines worldwide. Good News World Outreach owns WRNO Worldwide, a short-wave radio station which has been broadcasting for over 20 years.

For more information on our activities and events, please join us for our meetings.

Our goal is to bring you programming that is positive and life affirming. We constantly ask ourselves, what good news can we bring in a world full of bad news?

Good News
Good News is that the God that created the universe loves you as you are. He wants to give you power to become his child, just for the asking. The word of God says:

“But as many as received him, to them gave he power to become the sons of God, even to them that believe on his name.” John 1:12

God’s master plan for you is for Prosperity, Peace, and Joy. Good news is a lifestyle that begins by accepting Jesus as your personal Savior and Lord. You do not know what you are missing, if you do not know Jesus as your avior. Encounter your destiny. And start living the good news….

Then the rest of your life will become the best of your life.

World
The Bible says:

“For God so loved the world that he gave his only begotten son, that whosoever believeth in him should not perish, but have everlasting life.” John 3:16

We believe the current crises in the world exist, because the world does not know Jesus. To know Jesus is to have peace and everlasting life.

Outreach
WRNO Worldwide is an international short-wave radio station aking the whole word of God to the whole world. Although we cannot bring every person to Jesus, we can take Jesus to every person over the airwaves. We are dedicated to finishing this task in our generation.
