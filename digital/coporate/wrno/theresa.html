Theresa Lavarda

 
 



Theresa Lavarda has 28 years of strategic experience in business operations, business development, national strategy and growth in various business sectors including, banking, manufacturing, short wave radio broadcasting, insurance and information technology. Executive management positions of Assistant Vice President, Executive Director, Secretary/Treasurer and Chief Operating Officer, reporting directly to the CEO of several multi-million dollar companies.  Broad experience in strategic direction and leadership for the conception, development and implementation of policies, procedures, guidelines, and standards for sound information technology business and investment practices to meet the overall information technology strategy. Works closely with senior management in formulating strategies to maintain, enhance and improve current systems as well as proposing new methods of accomplishing the company’s overall goals through innovative ideas and information systems. Strong leadership ability, strong project management skills, and significant experience in tracking project activities and managing staff.

Managed a $20 million budget with state-of-the-art, cutting-edge information technology enterprise architecture, infrastructure, security systems development and integration, and telecommunications.

Ms. Lavarda has over 20 years experience in international business relations.  She has been working with WRNO Worldwide developing business and strategic alliances between Israel, Mexico, and the U.S.  In addition, Ms. Lavarda has been involved in
various activities related to governmental, humanitarian, and business development.

Theresa and her husband Michael of 30 years live in Fort Worth, Texas with their son Matthew.
